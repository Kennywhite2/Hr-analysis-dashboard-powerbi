# DAX Measures (HR Analytics)

Below are typical KPI definitions used in the dashboard (names may vary depending on your dataset).

## Headcount
**Definition:** Distinct count of employees (or active employees depending on the model).
- Example: `Headcount = DISTINCTCOUNT(HR[EmployeeID])`

## Attrition
**Definition:** Count of employees where Attrition = "Yes".
- Example: `Attrition = CALCULATE([Headcount], HR[Attrition] = "Yes")`

## Attrition Rate
**Definition:** Attrition / Headcount.
- Example: `Attrition Rate = DIVIDE([Attrition], [Headcount])`

## Active Employees
**Definition:** Employees where Attrition = "No".
- Example: `Active = CALCULATE([Headcount], HR[Attrition] = "No")`

## Average Age
**Definition:** Average age of employees.
- Example: `Avg Age = AVERAGE(HR[Age])`
